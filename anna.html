about anna

anna is Gili Estlin Hirsch's first collection of poetry, gathered during her time spent studying and traveling. It explores the personal journey of the individual and touches on topics such as love, loss, self-exploration, sexuality, LGBTQ experiences, and the notion of growing up. anna, in poetry form, sews together the pieces of a love story between two women. Although the poems are separate, the book as a whole embodies a complete narrative, examining the anatomy of this relationship from beginning to end.
