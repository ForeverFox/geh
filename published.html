published works

anna

Anna is an exploration in poem and in image of the raveling and unraveling of a passionate, tumultuous love story. Poet Gili Estlin Hirsch's words and artist Alex Ogden's illustrations follow two women as they weave together a powerful romance and are rocked by its force, through to its inevitable end.

Published in March 2014 by Fox & Owl Press

body of work

Body of Work is a bold and sensual photography series depicting poetry fragments written on the author's body, reflecting a desire to infuse the written word with living, visual elements.

Published in April 2014 by Fox & Owl Press
